FAQs

How do you store my keys?

We don't store your keys, they located in your Browser local storage, so we don't know them and can't restore. Remember: We do not store any user private data.

Can I stake/unstake my Dreamchain Tokens in your wallet?

Yes, you can perform these actions also as others: vote, delegate, create new accounts and much more. Just log in your account.

Do you have a mobile application?

No, currently we have created only a web browser application, but you can use on your mobile devices also.

Do you support Airdrops?

We support all major Dreamchain Tokens airdrops, contact us in telegram if we missed the good one.

I am a developer, can I use your code?

Yes, we have created open source software, so you can fork or update it.

Can I restore my Dreamchain Tokens wallet in another device?

Yes, you can restore your wallet in any device. You should only know your Private keys or be logged in Scatter.

Houston, we have a problem. Who can help me?

In case you have problems in connection to our wallet, work with accounts, managing your Dreamchain Tokens and others - just contact us via telegram, we would be happy to assist you.
